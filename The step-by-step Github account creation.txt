The step-by-step instructions to create a GitHub account on GitHub:

✅ Step 1: Visit GitHub Website


Open your browser.


Go to https://github.com



✅ Step 2: Click “Sign up”


On the homepage, click the Sign up button (top right corner).



✅ Step 3: Enter Your Details
You’ll be asked to provide:


Email address


Use a valid email (you’ll need to verify it).




Password


Choose a strong password (mix of letters, numbers & symbols).




Username


This will be your public identity (e.g., john-dev123).


It must be unique.




Click Continue after each step.

✅ Step 4: Verify Your Account


Complete the verification puzzle (to confirm you're not a robot).


Check your email inbox.


Click the verification link sent by GitHub.


🎉 Done!
Your GitHub account is now ready.

please keep note your credentials for future reference
